# Getting Started with Vystia

Welcome to Vystia - a custom Ultima Online shard with 26 unique classes, 12 magic schools, and a rich custom world.

---

## Starting Out

### 1. Talk to a Trainer
Find your class trainer to officially select your class. They're usually in the starting area or class-appropriate regions.

### 2. Get Your Equipment
When you select a class, you receive:
- Starting weapon and armor
- Class focus item (special ability item)
- Starter reagents (for magic classes)
- Spellbook (for magic classes)

### 3. Learn Your Abilities
- **Magic classes:** Open your spellbook. Spells are organized by circles 1-8.
- **Martial classes:** Check your abilities list. Use hotkeys to activate.

### 4. Start Quests
Talk to your trainer again to receive your first quest. Quests teach you class mechanics and reward items.

## Understanding Resources

### Magic Classes
- **Mana** - Standard casting resource
- **Reagents** - Consumed when casting spells (8 types per school)

### Martial Classes
Each class has a unique resource:

| Class | Resource | How It Works |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| Barbarian | Fury | Builds in combat, enables rage abilities |
| Monk | Chi | Builds with attacks, spent on finishers |
| Rogue | Combo Points | Build with attacks, spend on finishers |
| Fighter | Stamina | Regenerates, spent on abilities |
| Ranger | Focus | Builds while aiming, enhances shots |

## Combat Basics

### Casting Spells
1. Open spellbook
2. Double-click spell
3. Target (if required)
4. Wait for cast time
5. Reagents consumed on success

### Using Abilities
1. Activate ability (hotkey or gump)
2. Ability executes or you target
3. Resource consumed
4. Cooldown starts

### Stances
Many classes have stances - combat modes that change your abilities:
- Only one stance active at a time
- Switch stances based on situation
- Some abilities only work in certain stances

## Tips for New Players

1. **Complete your class quests** - They teach mechanics and give good rewards
2. **Learn your combos** - Classes have ability rotations for maximum effect
3. **Match your region** - Fighting in your class's region gives you advantages
4. **Group up** - Some content requires groups
5. **Experiment with stances** - Different situations call for different stances
6. **Keep reagents stocked** - Magic classes need reagents to cast
