# Vystia Classes Guide

Complete guide to all 26 classes.

---

## Magic Classes

### Ice Mage
**Region:** Frosthold | **Resource:** Chill | **School:** Ice Magic

Control the battlefield with freezing spells. Slow enemies, create barriers, and unleash devastating cold damage.

**Key Spells:**
- Circle 1-2: Frost Touch, Ice Shard, Frost Ward
- Circle 3-4: Frozen Ground, Icicle Barrage
- Circle 5-6: Shatter, Deep Freeze, Blizzard
- Circle 7-8: Frost Meteor, Ice Age

**Playstyle:** Control mage. Slow enemies, set up big damage.

---

### Druid
**Region:** Verdantpeak | **Resource:** Nature | **School:** Nature Magic

Master of nature and shapeshifting. Heal allies, control plants, transform into animals.

**Key Spells:**
- Healing: Rejuvenation, Nature's Blessing
- Offense: Thorn Spike, Entangling Roots
- Transform: Bear Form, Wolf Form
- Ultimate: Force of Nature

**Playstyle:** Versatile. Can heal, damage, or tank based on form.

---

### Witch
**Region:** Shadowfen | **Resource:** Hex | **School:** Hex Magic

Curse your enemies with debilitating hexes. Stack damage over time, weaken foes.

**Key Spells:**
- Curses: Weakness, Slow, Vulnerability
- DoTs: Poison Hex, Burning Hex
- Control: Fear, Polymorph
- Ultimate: Death Curse

**Playstyle:** Debuffer. Weaken enemies for your team.

---

### Sorcerer
**Region:** Emberlands | **Resource:** Heat | **School:** Elemental Magic

Pure elemental destruction. Fire, lightning, and explosive magic.

**Key Spells:**
- Fire: Fireball, Flame Strike, Meteor
- Lightning: Chain Lightning, Thunder
- Combined: Elemental Storm

**Playstyle:** Glass cannon. Maximum damage, low defense.

---

### Warlock
**Region:** ShadowVoid | **Resource:** Shadow | **School:** Dark Magic

Dark pacts and shadow magic. Drain life, corrupt enemies, summon demons.

**Key Spells:**
- Drains: Life Tap, Soul Drain
- DoTs: Corruption, Agony
- Demons: Summon Imp, Summon Demon
- Ultimate: Dark Pact

**Playstyle:** Sustain damage dealer. Heal yourself while hurting enemies.

---

### Oracle
**Region:** Crystal Barrens | **Resource:** Foresight | **School:** Divination

See the future, buff allies, counter enemies before they act.

**Key Spells:**
- Buffs: Foresight, Battle Insight
- Debuffs: Revealed Weakness
- Utility: Scrying, Prophecy
- Ultimate: Time Stop

**Playstyle:** Support. Empower allies, predict and counter threats.

---

### Necromancer
**Region:** ShadowVoid | **Resource:** Soul | **School:** Necromancy

Command the dead. Raise skeletons, drain life force, spread disease.

**Key Spells:**
- Summons: Raise Skeleton, Create Zombie
- Drains: Life Drain, Soul Harvest
- Disease: Plague, Decay
- Ultimate: Army of the Dead

**Playstyle:** Pet class with self-sustain and area damage.

---

### Summoner
**Region:** Underwater | **Resource:** Essence | **School:** Summoning

Summon powerful creatures to fight for you.

**Key Spells:**
- Elementals: Fire/Water/Earth/Air Elemental
- Beasts: Summon Wolf, Summon Bear
- Greater: Summon Phoenix, Summon Dragon
- Ultimate: Gate

**Playstyle:** Pet master. Your summons do the fighting.

---

### Shaman
**Region:** Multi | **Resource:** Spirit | **School:** Shamanic

Place totems, commune with spirits, heal and buff.

**Key Spells:**
- Totems: Healing Totem, Fire Totem, Earth Totem
- Spirits: Spirit Link, Ancestral Guidance
- Heals: Chain Heal, Spirit Mend
- Ultimate: Bloodlust

**Playstyle:** Support with totems. Good in groups.

---

### Bard
**Region:** Multi | **Resource:** Harmony | **School:** Bardic

Songs and performances that buff allies and debuff enemies.

**Key Spells:**
- Buffs: Song of Valor, Anthem of Strength
- Debuffs: Discordant Note, Lullaby
- Heals: Soothing Melody
- Ultimate: Symphony of War

**Playstyle:** Party support. Always have a song playing.

---

### Enchanter
**Region:** Multi | **Resource:** Mana | **School:** Enchanting

Enhance items and allies with magical properties.

**Key Spells:**
- Weapon: Flame Weapon, Frost Weapon
- Armor: Stoneskin, Magic Shield
- Ally: Haste, Invisibility
- Ultimate: Permanence

**Playstyle:** Buffer. Make allies and equipment stronger.

---

### Illusionist
**Region:** Desert | **Resource:** Mirage | **School:** Illusion

Deception and confusion. Create illusions, misdirect enemies.

**Key Spells:**
- Illusions: Mirror Image, Phantom
- Control: Confuse, Blind
- Escape: Invisibility, Blink
- Ultimate: Mass Confusion

**Playstyle:** Trickster. Control through deception.

---

## Martial Classes

### Fighter
**Resource:** Stamina | **Region:** Ironclad

Versatile warrior. Balanced offense and defense.

**Key Abilities:** Strike, Shield Bash, Charge, Whirlwind

---

### Barbarian
**Resource:** Fury | **Region:** Frosthold

Rage-fueled berserker. High damage when enraged.

**Key Abilities:** Rage, Cleave, Reckless Attack, Rampage
**Combo:** Build Fury → Activate Rage → Spam damage abilities

---

### Monk
**Resource:** Chi | **Region:** Ironclad

Martial artist with fast combos.

**Key Abilities:** Jab, Palm Strike, Flying Kick, Zen Focus
**Combo:** Build Chi with Jab → Spend on finishers

---

### Rogue
**Resource:** Combo Points | **Region:** Multi

Stealth and assassination.

**Key Abilities:** Stealth, Backstab, Eviscerate, Vanish
**Combo:** Stealth → Backstab → Build combo → Eviscerate

---

### Ranger
**Resource:** Focus | **Region:** Desert

Ranged combat and tracking.

**Key Abilities:** Aimed Shot, Multishot, Track, Trap

---

### Knight
**Resource:** Honor | **Region:** Multi

Mounted combat and defense.

**Key Abilities:** Charge, Shield Wall, Joust, Rally

---

### Paladin
**Resource:** Devotion | **Region:** Multi

Holy warrior with healing.

**Key Abilities:** Holy Strike, Lay on Hands, Divine Shield, Consecrate

---

### Templar
**Resource:** Zeal | **Region:** Ironclad

---

### Bounty Hunter

---

### Beastmaster
**Resource:** Bond | **Region:** Frosthold

Pet mastery.

---

### Artificer
**Resource:** Power | **Region:** Ironclad

Gadgets and constructs.

**Key Abilities:** Deploy Turret, Repair, Overclock, Bomb

---

### Alchemist
**Resource:** Reagent | **Region:** Verdantpeak

Potions and transmutation.

---

### Cleric
**Resource:** Faith | **Region:** Multi

Healing and protection.

**Key Abilities:** Heal, Protection, Smite, Resurrection

---

### Wizard
**Resource:** Arcane | **Region:** Crystal Barrens

Versatile magic.

**Key Abilities:** Arcane Blast, Mana Shield, Teleport, Arcane Storm
