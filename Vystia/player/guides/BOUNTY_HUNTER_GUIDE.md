# Bounty Hunter - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Bounty Hunter
**Region:** Multi-Regional
**Role:** Ranged / Melee
**Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆ (Intermediate)

---

## Overview

Tracking, traps, bounties, assassination. Hunt and eliminate marked targets with precision.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 30 |
| DEX | 35 |
| INT | 15 |

### Primary Skill
**Manhunting (ID: 78)** - Governs all Bounty Hunter abilities

**Special Item:** BountyLedger

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Hunt and eliminate marked targets with precision

### Strengths
- Target marking
- Tracking
- Burst damage on marked
- Versatile combat

### Weaknesses
- Setup required
- Mark cooldowns
- Less group utility
- Single target focus

---

## Stance System

Bounty Hunters have access to the following stances:

- **Hunter Stance** (+tracking)
- **Assassin Stance** (+burst)
- **Survivor Stance** (+defense)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance hunter]
[stance assassin]
[stance survivor]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Mark Target
- **Effect:** Mark for bonus damage
- **Cooldown:** 30s cooldown

#### Execute Contract
- **Effect:** Massive damage to marked
- **Cooldown:** 60s cooldown

#### Track Prey
- **Effect:** Reveal marked target location
- **Cooldown:** 45s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Track and find target
2. Mark Target for bonus
3. Engage with traps
4. Burst damage combo
5. Execute Contract finish
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Ranged / Melee means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay at range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset

## Leveling Path

### Levels 1-20
