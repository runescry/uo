# Cleric - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Cleric
**Region:** Multi-Regional
**Role:** Healer
**Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆ (Easy)

---

## Overview

Divine healing, holy magic, protection, blessings. Dedicated healer with powerful divine magic.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 20 |
| DEX | 20 |
| INT | 40 |

### Primary Skill
**DivineGrace (ID: 82)** - Governs all Cleric abilities

**Special Item:** HolySymbol

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Dedicated healer with powerful divine magic

### Strengths
- Best single target heals
- Divine buffs
- Resurrection
- Anti-undead

### Weaknesses
- Low damage
- Heal focused
- Target of enemies
- Mana management

---

## Stance System

Clerics have access to the following stances:

- **Holy Stance** (+healing)
- **Protection Stance** (+shields)
- **Smite Stance** (+vs undead)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance holy]
[stance protection]
[stance smite]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Divine Heal
- **Effect:** Powerful single heal
- **Cooldown:** 10s cooldown

#### Resurrection
- **Effect:** Revive dead ally
- **Cooldown:** 300s cooldown

#### Holy Nova
- **Effect:** AoE heal + damage undead
- **Cooldown:** 30s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Bless party before fight
2. Shield tank
3. Heal priority targets
4. Holy Nova for AoE heal
5. Resurrect fallen
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Healer means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay in melee range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
