# Ice Mage - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Ice Mage
**Region:** Frosthold
**Role:** Caster DPS / Crowd Control
**Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆ (Intermediate)

---

## Overview

The Ice Mage harnesses the frozen powers of Frosthold, combining devastating cold damage with powerful crowd control. Masters of kiting and area denial, Ice Mages excel at controlling the battlefield while dealing consistent damage from range.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value | Bonus |
|------|-------|-------|
| STR | 15 | - |
| DEX | 20 | - |
| INT | 45 | +15% mana pool |

### Primary Skill
**Cryomancy (ID: 58)** - Governs all Ice Magic spell effectiveness

### Spellbook
**Tome of Frozen Arts** (IceMageSpellbook)
- 32 spells across 8 circles
- Spell IDs: 1000-1031
- Spawn: `[spellbook ice]` or `[sb ice]`

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concepts

1. **Kiting** - Keep enemies at range using slows and freezes
2. **Area Control** - Block paths with ice walls and frozen ground
3. **Burst Windows** - Save big spells for when enemies are grouped
4. **Resource Management** - High circle spells are mana-intensive

### Combat Loop

```
1. Open with Chill Aura (self-buff, slows nearby enemies)
2. Apply Ice Shield for protection
3. Kite with Ice Shard / Freezing Grasp (damage + slow)
4. When enemies group: Blizzard / Avalanche (AoE)
5. Emergency: Frozen Tomb (hard CC) or Ice Wall (escape)
6. Execute: Rime Reaper on low-health targets
```

## Stance System

Ice Mages have access to **Caster Stances** that modify their playstyle:

### Frost Stance (Default)
- Balanced cold damage and control
- Standard mana costs
- Normal cast times

### Glacial Stance
- +20% cold damage
- -10% cast speed
- Best for: Burst damage phases

### Permafrost Stance
- +30% slow/freeze duration
- -15% damage
- Best for: Crowd control focus

### Blizzard Stance
- +25% AoE radius
- +20% mana cost
- Best for: Large group fights

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance frost]
[stance glacial]
[stance permafrost]
[stance blizzard]
```

## Ability System

### Passive Abilities

**Cold Mastery**
- Unlocks at Cryomancy 50
- +10% cold damage permanently

**Frost Affinity**
- Unlocks at Cryomancy 80
- +15% cold resistance
- Immune to slow effects

**Winter's Heart**
- Unlocks at Cryomancy 100
- Killing blows restore 5% mana

### Active Abilities

**Flash Freeze** (Cooldown: 60s)
- Instantly freeze all enemies within 4 tiles
- Duration: 3 seconds
- Command: `[ability flashfreeze]`

**Glacial Dash** (Cooldown: 30s)
- Teleport up to 6 tiles, leaving ice trail
- Trail slows enemies
- Command: `[ability glacialdash]`

**Ice Block** (Cooldown: 120s)
- Encase yourself in ice
- Immune to all damage for 5 seconds
- Cannot act while encased
- Command: `[ability iceblock]`

## Spell Rotation Examples

### Solo Leveling Rotation
```
1. Frost Armor (maintain always)
2. Ice Shield (before each pull)
3. Chill Aura (start of combat)
4. Ice Shard spam (damage)
5. Freezing Grasp (if enemy reaches you)
6. Repeat Ice Shard until dead
```

### Group Dungeon Rotation
```
1. Frost Armor + Ice Shield (pre-buff)
2. Wait for tank to pull
3. Frozen Ground (slow zone on enemies)
4. Blizzard (AoE damage)
5. Ice Shard (filler)
6. Frost Meteor (when grouped)
7. Deep Freeze (interrupt casters)
```

### Boss Fight Rotation
```
1. All buffs up before pull
2. Ice Age (slow aura)
3. Glacial Strike (opener)
4. Frostbite (DoT)
5. Icicle Barrage (burst)
6. Ice Shard spam (sustain)
7. Deep Freeze (add control)
8. Rime Reaper (execute at 20%)
```

### Emergency Survival
```
1. Frozen Tomb (on dangerous enemy)
2. Ice Wall (block pursuit)
3. Glacial Dash (escape ability)
4. Ice Block (if about to die)
5. Glacial Mend (heal when safe)
```

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Always kite** - You're squishy, keep enemies at range
2. **Pre-buff** - Frost Armor and Ice Shield before every fight
3. **Watch mana** - High circle spells drain fast
4. **Use terrain** - Ice Wall in doorways = easy kills

### Advanced Techniques

**Shatter Combo**
- Deep Freeze → Shatter = massive single target damage
- Only works on frozen targets

**AoE Trap**
- Ice Wall behind enemies → Blizzard on them
- They can't escape the damage

**Kite Train**
- Chill Aura + Frost Slick + run backwards
- Enemies can never catch you

**Execute Fishing**
- Get enemy to ~25% → wait for 20% → Rime Reaper
- Instant kill saves time

## Counters & Weaknesses

### Weak Against
- **Fire mages** - Your cold resist means nothing
- **Fast melee** - Gap closers ignore your slows
- **Reflect effects** - Your damage hurts you
- **Dispels** - Removes your shields and buffs

### Strong Against
- **Slow melee** - Kite forever
- **Grouped enemies** - AoE dominance
- **Casters** - Deep Freeze interrupts
- **Cold creatures** - High cold resist

## Leveling Path

### Levels 1-20
- Focus on Ice Shard and Chill Aura
- Learn to kite effectively
- Practice mana management

### Levels 21-40
- Add Ice Shield and Frost Armor
- Start using Blizzard for groups
- Learn crowd control timing

### Levels 41-60
- Master Shatter combos
- Use Circle 7 spells
- Practice stance switching

### Levels 61+
- Full rotation mastery
- Absolute Zero timing
- Rime Reaper executes
