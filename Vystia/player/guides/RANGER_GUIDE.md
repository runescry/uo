# Ranger - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Ranger
**Region:** Desert
**Role:** Ranged DPS
**Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆ (Easy)

---

## Overview

Archery, traps, tracking, survival. Mobile ranged attacker with trap utility.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 25 |
| DEX | 45 |
| INT | 10 |

### Primary Skill
**Marksmanship (ID: 75)** - Governs all Ranger abilities


---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Mobile ranged attacker with trap utility

### Strengths
- High sustained ranged damage
- Traps for control
- Good mobility
- Tracking abilities

### Weaknesses
- Limited magic
- Weak in melee
- Trap setup time
- Arrow management

---

## Stance System

Rangers have access to the following stances:

- **Sniper Stance** (+damage, -speed)
- **Rapid Fire Stance** (+speed, -damage)
- **Survival Stance** (+defense, mobility)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance sniper]
[stance rapid]
[stance survival]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Aimed Shot
- **Effect:** Powerful charged shot
- **Cooldown:** 15s cooldown

#### Multi-Shot
- **Effect:** Hit multiple targets
- **Cooldown:** 20s cooldown

#### Bear Trap
- **Effect:** Root enemy for 4s
- **Cooldown:** 30s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Set traps at chokepoints
2. Open with Aimed Shot
3. Kite with normal shots
4. Multi-Shot for groups
5. Trap pursuers
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Ranged DPS means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay at range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
