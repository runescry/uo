# Illusionist - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Illusionist
**Region:** Desert
**Role:** Caster CC
**Difficulty:** ★★★★☆ (Hard)

---

## Overview

Illusions, misdirection, mind control, phantasms. Control and confuse enemies with illusions.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 10 |
| DEX | 25 |
| INT | 45 |

### Primary Skill
**IllusionMagic (ID: 69)** - Governs all Illusionist abilities

### Spellbook
**IllusionistSpellbook**
- 32 spells across 8 circles
- Spawn: `[spellbook illusionist]` or `[sb illusionist]`

**Special Item:** IllusionistSpellbook

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Control and confuse enemies with illusions

### Strengths
- Unmatched crowd control
- Escape abilities
- Confusion effects
- Mirror images

### Weaknesses
- Low direct damage
- Complex mechanics
- True sight counters
- Mana hungry

---

## Stance System

Illusionists have access to the following stances:

- **Phantom Stance** (+illusion duration)
- **Mind Stance** (+control effects)
- **Mirage Stance** (+evasion)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance phantom]
[stance mind]
[stance mirage]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Mirror Image
- **Effect:** Create 3 copies of yourself
- **Cooldown:** 60s cooldown

#### Mass Confusion
- **Effect:** Enemies attack each other
- **Cooldown:** 90s cooldown

#### Vanish
- **Effect:** Become invisible for 10s
- **Cooldown:** 45s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Mirror Image for safety
2. Confusion on groups
3. Phantasm for distraction
4. Control priority targets
5. Vanish if in danger
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Caster CC means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay at range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
