# Necromancer - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Necromancer
**Region:** ShadowVoid
**Role:** Caster / Pet
**Difficulty:** ★★★★☆ (Hard)

---

## Overview

Undead summoning, death magic, life drain, decay. Command undead armies while dealing death magic.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 15 |
| DEX | 15 |
| INT | 50 |

### Primary Skill
**NecromancyArts (ID: 60)** - Governs all Necromancer abilities

### Spellbook
**NecromancerSpellbook**
- 32 spells across 8 circles
- Spawn: `[spellbook necromancer]` or `[sb necromancer]`

**Special Item:** NecromancerSpellbook

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Command undead armies while dealing death magic

### Strengths
- Multiple pets
- Life drain sustain
- Corpse explosion
- Fear effects

### Weaknesses
- Needs corpses
- Weak to holy
- Pet management
- Ethical concerns

---

## Stance System

Necromancers have access to the following stances:

- **Unholy Stance** (+pet power)
- **Death Stance** (+damage)
- **Blood Stance** (+life drain)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance unholy]
[stance death]
[stance blood]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Raise Dead
- **Effect:** Create skeleton from corpse
- **Cooldown:** 30s cooldown

#### Army of Dead
- **Effect:** Raise multiple skeletons
- **Cooldown:** 120s cooldown

#### Death Coil
- **Effect:** Damage enemy, heal you
- **Cooldown:** 15s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Kill weak enemy for corpse
2. Raise Dead army
3. Death Coil for sustain
4. DoTs on targets
5. Corpse Explosion finish
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Caster / Pet means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay at range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
