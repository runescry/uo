# Alchemist - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Alchemist
**Region:** Verdantpeak
**Role:** Support
**Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆ (Intermediate)

---

## Overview

Potions, transmutation, bombs, elixirs. Support allies with potions and debuff enemies with bombs.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 20 |
| DEX | 30 |
| INT | 30 |

### Primary Skill
**Transmutation (ID: 81)** - Governs all Alchemist abilities

**Special Item:** AlchemistKit

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Support allies with potions and debuff enemies with bombs

### Strengths
- Strong buffs
- Versatile utility
- Potion crafting
- AoE bombs

### Weaknesses
- Consumable dependent
- Setup time
- Limited direct combat
- Resource management

---

## Stance System

Alchemists have access to the following stances:

- **Brewer Stance** (+potion power)
- **Bomber Stance** (+explosive damage)
- **Transmuter Stance** (+buff duration)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance brewer]
[stance bomber]
[stance transmuter]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Quick Brew
- **Effect:** Instantly create a potion
- **Cooldown:** 30s cooldown

#### Volatile Mixture
- **Effect:** Throw AoE damage bomb
- **Cooldown:** 20s cooldown

#### Elixir of Power
- **Effect:** Major buff to target
- **Cooldown:** 60s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Pre-buff party with elixirs
2. Volatile Mixture opener
3. Support with potions
4. Bombs for AoE
5. Transmute as needed
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Support means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay in melee range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
