# Beastmaster - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Beastmaster
**Region:** Frosthold
**Role:** Pet / Ranged
**Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆ (Intermediate)

---

## Overview

Animal companions, nature bond, ranged attacks with pet support. Command pets while dealing ranged damage.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 25 |
| DEX | 35 |
| INT | 20 |

### Primary Skill
**BeastBonding (ID: 79)** - Governs all Beastmaster abilities

**Special Item:** BeastWhistle

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Command pets while dealing ranged damage

### Strengths
- Pet tanking
- Versatile pet types
- Good solo potential
- Ranged safety

### Weaknesses
- Pet management complexity
- Weak without pet
- Pet can die
- Split attention

---

## Stance System

Beastmasters have access to the following stances:

- **Pack Leader Stance** (+pet damage)
- **Survival Stance** (+pet defense)
- **Hunter Stance** (+ranged damage)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance pack]
[stance survival]
[stance hunter]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Call of the Wild
- **Effect:** Summon temporary beast
- **Cooldown:** 120s cooldown

#### Beast Bond
- **Effect:** Heal your pet significantly
- **Cooldown:** 30s cooldown

#### Feral Rage
- **Effect:** Pet deals double damage for 10s
- **Cooldown:** 60s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Summon/command pet to attack
2. Ranged attacks from safety
3. Heal pet as needed
4. Use Feral Rage for burst
5. Call of Wild for tough fights
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Pet / Ranged means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay at range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset

## Leveling Path

### Levels 1-20
