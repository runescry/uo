# Warlock - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Warlock
**Region:** ShadowVoid
**Role:** Caster DPS
**Difficulty:** ★★★★☆ (Hard)

---

## Overview

Demons, shadow magic, chaos, dark pacts. Summon demons and unleash shadow destruction.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 15 |
| DEX | 20 |
| INT | 45 |

### Primary Skill
**Demonology (ID: 59)** - Governs all Warlock abilities

### Spellbook
**WarlockSpellbook**
- 32 spells across 8 circles
- Spawn: `[spellbook warlock]` or `[sb warlock]`

**Special Item:** WarlockSpellbook

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Summon demons and unleash shadow destruction

### Strengths
- Demon pets
- High shadow damage
- Fear effects
- Chaos magic

### Weaknesses
- Self-damage from pacts
- Complex pet management
- Long cooldowns
- Weak to holy

---

## Stance System

Warlocks have access to the following stances:

- **Destruction Stance** (+damage)
- **Demonology Stance** (+pet power)
- **Affliction Stance** (+DoTs)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance destruction]
[stance demonology]
[stance affliction]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Summon Demon
- **Effect:** Call forth a demon minion
- **Cooldown:** 180s cooldown

#### Dark Pact
- **Effect:** Sacrifice HP for massive damage
- **Cooldown:** 60s cooldown

#### Demonic Form
- **Effect:** Transform into demon
- **Cooldown:** 300s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Summon demon first
2. Apply corruption DoTs
3. Shadow bolts for damage
4. Dark Pact for burst
5. Fear fleeing enemies
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Caster DPS means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay at range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
