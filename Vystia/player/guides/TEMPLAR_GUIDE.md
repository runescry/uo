# Templar - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Templar
**Region:** Ironclad
**Role:** Tank / DPS
**Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆ (Intermediate)

---

## Overview

Holy warrior, divine punishment, righteous fury. Holy warrior dealing divine damage while tanking.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 35 |
| DEX | 25 |
| INT | 20 |

### Primary Skill
**Zealotry (ID: 77)** - Governs all Templar abilities

**Special Item:** TemplarCross

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Holy warrior dealing divine damage while tanking

### Strengths
- Holy damage
- Self-buffs
- Anti-undead
- Hybrid tank/DPS

### Weaknesses
- Split role
- Zealotry resource
- Moderate at both roles
- Limited healing

---

## Stance System

Templars have access to the following stances:

- **Crusader Stance** (+holy damage)
- **Guardian Stance** (+defense)
- **Zealot Stance** (+zealotry gain)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance crusader]
[stance guardian]
[stance zealot]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Divine Strike
- **Effect:** Holy damage attack
- **Cooldown:** 10s cooldown

#### Holy Shield
- **Effect:** Block + reflect holy
- **Cooldown:** 30s cooldown

#### Smite
- **Effect:** High damage vs undead/demons
- **Cooldown:** 20s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Charge with Divine Strike
2. Holy Shield for defense
3. Smite undead/demons
4. Build zealotry
5. Execute with holy burst
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Tank / DPS means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay in melee range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
