# Sorcerer - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Sorcerer
**Region:** Emberlands
**Role:** Caster DPS
**Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆ (Intermediate)

---

## Overview

Fire, earth, air, water elemental magic. Versatile elemental caster with multiple damage types.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 15 |
| DEX | 20 |
| INT | 45 |

### Primary Skill
**Elementalism (ID: 62)** - Governs all Sorcerer abilities

### Spellbook
**SorcererSpellbook**
- 32 spells across 8 circles
- Spawn: `[spellbook sorcerer]` or `[sb sorcerer]`

**Special Item:** SorcererSpellbook

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Versatile elemental caster with multiple damage types

### Strengths
- Multiple damage types
- Strong AoE
- Elemental shields
- Bypass specific resists

### Weaknesses
- Mana intensive
- Squishy
- Long cast times
- Element matching needed

---

## Stance System

Sorcerers have access to the following stances:

- **Fire Stance** (+fire damage)
- **Earth Stance** (+defense)
- **Storm Stance** (+lightning)
- **Water Stance** (+healing)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance fire]
[stance earth]
[stance storm]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Elemental Attunement
- **Effect:** Switch active element instantly
- **Cooldown:** 10s cooldown

#### Elemental Surge
- **Effect:** Empower next spell 50%
- **Cooldown:** 45s cooldown

#### Primordial Form
- **Effect:** Transform into elemental for 20s
- **Cooldown:** 180s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Attune to target weakness
2. Shield up before combat
3. Open with empowered spell
4. AoE for groups
5. Switch elements as needed
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Caster DPS means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay at range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
