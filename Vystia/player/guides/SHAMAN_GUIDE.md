# Shaman - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Shaman
**Region:** Multi-Regional
**Role:** Healer / Hybrid
**Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆ (Intermediate)

---

## Overview

Spirits, totems, lightning, ancestral magic. Call upon spirits and totems to heal and damage.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 20 |
| DEX | 20 |
| INT | 40 |

### Primary Skill
**SpiritCalling (ID: 67)** - Governs all Shaman abilities

### Spellbook
**ShamanSpellbook**
- 32 spells across 8 circles
- Spawn: `[spellbook shaman]` or `[sb shaman]`

**Special Item:** ShamanSpellbook, SpiritTotem

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Call upon spirits and totems to heal and damage

### Strengths
- Totem utility
- Spirit healing
- Lightning damage
- Versatile support

### Weaknesses
- Totem positioning
- Spirit management
- Mobile enemies avoid totems
- Split focus

---

## Stance System

Shamans have access to the following stances:

- **Spirit Stance** (+healing)
- **Storm Stance** (+lightning)
- **Totem Stance** (+totem power)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance spirit]
[stance storm]
[stance totem]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Spirit Link
- **Effect:** Share damage among party
- **Cooldown:** 60s cooldown

#### Lightning Storm
- **Effect:** AoE lightning damage
- **Cooldown:** 30s cooldown

#### Ancestral Spirit
- **Effect:** Summon healing spirit
- **Cooldown:** 90s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Place totems strategically
2. Spirit Link on tank
3. Heal with spirit spells
4. Lightning Storm damage
5. Ancestral Spirit emergency
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Healer / Hybrid means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay in melee range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
