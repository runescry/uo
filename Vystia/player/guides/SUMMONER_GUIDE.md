# Summoner - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Summoner
**Region:** Underwater
**Role:** Pet / Caster
**Difficulty:** ★★★★☆ (Hard)

---

## Overview

Creature summoning, planar bonds, elemental conjuration. Summon and command powerful elemental creatures.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 15 |
| DEX | 20 |
| INT | 45 |

### Primary Skill
**Conjuration (ID: 66)** - Governs all Summoner abilities

### Spellbook
**SummonerSpellbook**
- 32 spells across 8 circles
- Spawn: `[spellbook summoner]` or `[sb summoner]`

**Special Item:** SummonerSpellbook, SummoningCircle

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Summon and command powerful elemental creatures

### Strengths
- Powerful summons
- Elemental variety
- Strong pets
- Planar magic

### Weaknesses
- Summon limits
- Cast times
- Mana intensive
- Pet deaths hurt

---

## Stance System

Summoners have access to the following stances:

- **Conjurer Stance** (+summon duration)
- **Binder Stance** (+summon power)
- **Elemental Stance** (+elemental damage)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance conjurer]
[stance binder]
[stance elemental]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Greater Summon
- **Effect:** Call powerful creature
- **Cooldown:** 180s cooldown

#### Planar Binding
- **Effect:** Strengthen summon greatly
- **Cooldown:** 60s cooldown

#### Dismiss
- **Effect:** Explode summon for AoE
- **Cooldown:** 30s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Summon before combat
2. Planar Binding buff
3. Support summon with spells
4. Dismiss for AoE burst
5. Re-summon as needed
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Pet / Caster means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay at range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
