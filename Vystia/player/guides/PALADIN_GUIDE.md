# Paladin - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Paladin
**Region:** Multi-Regional
**Role:** Tank / Healer
**Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆ (Intermediate)

---

## Overview

Holy knight, protection, healing, divine justice. Holy tank who can heal and deal divine damage.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 35 |
| DEX | 20 |
| INT | 25 |

### Primary Skill
**HolyDevotion (ID: 74)** - Governs all Paladin abilities


---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Holy tank who can heal and deal divine damage

### Strengths
- Self-healing tank
- Divine damage
- Auras
- Anti-evil

### Weaknesses
- Split stats
- Moderate at both roles
- Holy restrictions
- Mana limits

---

## Stance System

Paladins have access to the following stances:

- **Devotion Stance** (+healing aura)
- **Retribution Stance** (+damage)
- **Protection Stance** (+defense)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance devotion]
[stance retribution]
[stance protection]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Lay on Hands
- **Effect:** Full heal on target
- **Cooldown:** 600s cooldown

#### Divine Shield
- **Effect:** Invulnerable for 8s
- **Cooldown:** 300s cooldown

#### Consecration
- **Effect:** Holy ground damage
- **Cooldown:** 15s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Aura active always
2. Consecration on pull
3. Self-heal as needed
4. Divine Shield emergency
5. Lay on Hands saves
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Tank / Healer means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay in melee range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
