# Artificer - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Artificer
**Region:** Ironclad
**Role:** Pet / Ranged
**Difficulty:** ★★★★☆ (Hard)

---

## Overview

Clockwork constructs, gadgets, turrets, steam power. Deploy constructs and gadgets to control the battlefield.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 25 |
| DEX | 30 |
| INT | 25 |

### Primary Skill
**Engineering (ID: 80)** - Governs all Artificer abilities

**Special Item:** ConstructControlDevice, ArtificerBlueprints

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Deploy constructs and gadgets to control the battlefield

### Strengths
- Multiple pets/turrets
- Gadget utility
- Zone control
- Mechanical allies

### Weaknesses
- Setup time
- Constructs can be destroyed
- Resource intensive
- Complex management

---

## Stance System

Artificers have access to the following stances:

- **Constructor Stance** (+pet power)
- **Gadgeteer Stance** (+gadget effects)
- **Engineer Stance** (+turret damage)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance constructor]
[stance gadgeteer]
[stance engineer]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Deploy Turret
- **Effect:** Place an auto-attacking turret
- **Cooldown:** 45s cooldown

#### Clockwork Companion
- **Effect:** Summon combat construct
- **Cooldown:** 120s cooldown

#### Overcharge
- **Effect:** Empower all constructs for 15s
- **Cooldown:** 90s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Deploy turrets at chokepoints
2. Summon construct
3. Gadgets for utility
4. Overcharge for burst
5. Repair constructs
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Pet / Ranged means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay at range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
