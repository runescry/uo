# Enchanter - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Enchanter
**Region:** Multi-Regional
**Role:** Utility / Buff
**Difficulty:** ★★★★☆ (Hard)

---

## Overview

Enchantments, runes, item enhancement, magical buffs. Enhance allies and items with powerful enchantments.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 15 |
| DEX | 25 |
| INT | 40 |

### Primary Skill
**Runeweaving (ID: 68)** - Governs all Enchanter abilities

### Spellbook
**EnchanterSpellbook**
- 32 spells across 8 circles
- Spawn: `[spellbook enchanter]` or `[sb enchanter]`

**Special Item:** EnchanterSpellbook, EnchantingCrystal

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Enhance allies and items with powerful enchantments

### Strengths
- Item enhancement
- Powerful buffs
- Rune magic
- Party empowerment

### Weaknesses
- Setup time
- Buff management
- Low direct damage
- Resource intensive

---

## Stance System

Enchanters have access to the following stances:

- **Enchant Stance** (+buff power)
- **Rune Stance** (+rune effects)
- **Empower Stance** (+item enhancement)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance enchant]
[stance rune]
[stance empower]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Greater Enchant
- **Effect:** Major weapon buff
- **Cooldown:** 60s cooldown

#### Rune of Power
- **Effect:** Place empowering rune
- **Cooldown:** 45s cooldown

#### Mass Enchant
- **Effect:** Buff entire party
- **Cooldown:** 120s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Enchant party weapons
2. Place power runes
3. Maintain buffs
4. Rune damage/utility
5. Re-enchant as needed
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Utility / Buff means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay in melee range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
