# Monk - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Monk
**Region:** Ironclad
**Role:** Melee / Hybrid
**Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆ (Intermediate)

---

## Overview

Martial arts, chi, meditation, unarmed combat. Fast unarmed striker with chi-powered abilities.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 30 |
| DEX | 35 |
| INT | 15 |

### Primary Skill
**MartialArts (ID: 72)** - Governs all Monk abilities

**Special Item:** MonkBeads

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Fast unarmed striker with chi-powered abilities

### Strengths
- Fast attacks
- Chi resource
- Evasion
- Self-healing through chi

### Weaknesses
- Unarmed only
- Squishy if hit
- Chi management
- Close range

---

## Stance System

Monks have access to the following stances:

- **Tiger Stance** (+damage)
- **Crane Stance** (+evasion)
- **Dragon Stance** (+chi regen)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance tiger]
[stance crane]
[stance dragon]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Flurry of Blows
- **Effect:** Rapid 5-hit combo
- **Cooldown:** 15s cooldown

#### Chi Heal
- **Effect:** Convert chi to health
- **Cooldown:** 20s cooldown

#### Flying Kick
- **Effect:** Gap closer with stun
- **Cooldown:** 25s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Flying Kick to engage
2. Build chi with basics
3. Flurry of Blows burst
4. Chi Heal when needed
5. Evade heavy attacks
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Melee / Hybrid means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay in melee range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset

## Leveling Path

### Levels 1-20
- Learn basic abilities
- Practice melee  fundamentals

### Levels 21-40

### Levels 41-60
