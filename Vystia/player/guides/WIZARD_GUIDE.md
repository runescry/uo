# Wizard - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Wizard
**Region:** Crystal Barrens
**Role:** Utility
**Difficulty:** ★★★★☆ (Hard)

---

## Overview

Arcane research, spell modification, magical theory. Modify and enhance spells through arcane knowledge.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 10 |
| DEX | 20 |
| INT | 50 |

### Primary Skill
**ArcaneStudies (ID: 83)** - Governs all Wizard abilities


---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Modify and enhance spells through arcane knowledge

### Strengths
- Spell modification
- Arcane utility
- Research bonuses
- Flexible magic

### Weaknesses
- Study requirements
- Complex mechanics
- Less direct power
- Knowledge gated

---

## Stance System

Wizards have access to the following stances:

- **Research Stance** (+learning)
- **Combat Stance** (+damage)
- **Utility Stance** (+spell effects)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance research]
[stance combat]
[stance utility]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Arcane Insight
- **Effect:** Analyze enemy weaknesses
- **Cooldown:** 30s cooldown

#### Spell Modification
- **Effect:** Enhance next spell
- **Cooldown:** 20s cooldown

#### Arcane Explosion
- **Effect:** Raw arcane damage
- **Cooldown:** 45s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Arcane Insight to analyze
2. Modify spells for weakness
3. Arcane Explosion burst
4. Utility spells support
5. Adapt to situation
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Utility means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay in melee range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset

---

## Leveling Path

### Levels 1-20
- Learn basic abilities

- Understand resource management

### Levels 21-40
- Add more abilities to rotation

- Group content practice

### Levels 41-60

- Advanced stance switching
