# Barbarian - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Barbarian
**Region:** Frosthold
**Role:** Melee DPS
**Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆ (Easy)

---

## Overview

Rage, fury, powerful melee attacks, self-buffs. Aggressive melee combat with rage mechanics.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 45 |
| DEX | 20 |
| INT | 15 |

### Primary Skill
**Berserking (ID: 70)** - Governs all Barbarian abilities

**Special Item:** RageTotem

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Aggressive melee combat with rage mechanics

### Strengths
- High burst damage
- Self-healing through combat
- Rage generation
- Strong vs single targets

### Weaknesses
- No ranged attacks
- Vulnerable to kiting
- Limited crowd control
- Rage depletes out of combat

---

## Stance System

Barbarians have access to the following stances:

- **Berserker Stance** (+damage, -defense)
- **Defensive Stance** (+defense, -damage)
- **Battle Stance** (balanced)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance berserker]
[stance defensive]
[stance battle]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Enrage
- **Effect:** Instantly gain 50 rage
- **Cooldown:** 60s cooldown

#### Whirlwind
- **Effect:** AoE spin attack
- **Cooldown:** Costs 30 rage

#### Execute
- **Effect:** High damage vs low HP targets
- **Cooldown:** Costs 20 rage


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Charge to engage
2. Build rage with basic attacks
3. Enrage when needed
4. Whirlwind for groups
5. Execute at <20% HP
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Melee DPS means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay in melee range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
