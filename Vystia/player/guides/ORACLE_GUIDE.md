# Oracle - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Oracle
**Region:** Crystal Barrens
**Role:** Utility
**Difficulty:** ★★★★★ (Expert)

---

## Overview

Prophecy, time manipulation, fate, foresight. Manipulate time and fate to control battle outcomes.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 10 |
| DEX | 20 |
| INT | 50 |

### Primary Skill
**Divination (ID: 65)** - Governs all Oracle abilities

### Spellbook
**OracleSpellbook**
- 32 spells across 8 circles
- Spawn: `[spellbook oracle]` or `[sb oracle]`

**Special Item:** OracleSpellbook, CrystalOrb

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Manipulate time and fate to control battle outcomes

### Strengths
- Time manipulation
- Fate changing
- Precognition
- Unique utility

### Weaknesses
- Very complex
- Low damage
- Support focused
- High skill cap

---

## Stance System

Oracles have access to the following stances:

- **Seer Stance** (+divination)
- **Chrono Stance** (+time effects)
- **Fate Stance** (+luck manipulation)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance seer]
[stance chrono]
[stance fate]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Foresight
- **Effect:** See next 3 enemy actions
- **Cooldown:** 60s cooldown

#### Time Rewind
- **Effect:** Undo last 5 seconds
- **Cooldown:** 180s cooldown

#### Alter Fate
- **Effect:** Change a failed roll to success
- **Cooldown:** 120s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Foresight to plan
2. Buff allies with fortune
3. Debuff enemies with ill fate
4. Time Rewind emergencies
5. Alter Fate critical moments
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Utility means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay in melee range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
