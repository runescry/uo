# Bard - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Bard
**Region:** Multi-Regional
**Role:** Support / CC
**Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆ (Intermediate)

---

## Overview

Music, songs, inspiration, crowd control. Inspire allies and debilitate enemies with music.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 15 |
| DEX | 35 |
| INT | 30 |

### Primary Skill
**BardicLore (ID: 63)** - Governs all Bard abilities

### Spellbook
**BardSpellbook**
- 32 spells across 8 circles
- Spawn: `[spellbook bard]` or `[sb bard]`

**Special Item:** BardSpellbook, MagicLute

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Inspire allies and debilitate enemies with music

### Strengths
- Party buffs
- Enemy debuffs
- Crowd control
- Versatile support

### Weaknesses
- Song management
- Interrupt vulnerable
- Low solo damage
- Support dependent

---

## Stance System

Bards have access to the following stances:

- **War Song Stance** (+party damage)
- **Ballad Stance** (+party defense)
- **Lullaby Stance** (+CC)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance war]
[stance ballad]
[stance lullaby]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Inspiring Song
- **Effect:** Buff all party stats
- **Cooldown:** 60s cooldown

#### Discordant Note
- **Effect:** AoE confusion
- **Cooldown:** 30s cooldown

#### Battle Hymn
- **Effect:** Major party damage buff
- **Cooldown:** 120s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Inspiring Song pre-fight
2. Battle Hymn on pull
3. Maintain buff songs
4. Discordant Note CC
5. Debuff priority targets
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Support / CC means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay in melee range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset

### Levels 1-20
