# Knight - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Knight
**Region:** Multi-Regional
**Role:** Tank / Melee
**Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆ (Easy)

---

## Overview

Chivalry, honor, mounted combat, leadership. Honorable warrior with party leadership abilities.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 38 |
| DEX | 27 |
| INT | 15 |

### Primary Skill
**ChivalricArts (ID: 73)** - Governs all Knight abilities

**Special Item:** KnightBanner

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Honorable warrior with party leadership abilities

### Strengths
- Party buffs
- Mounted combat
- Honor mechanics
- Reliable tank

### Weaknesses
- Honor restrictions
- Mount dependency
- Limited magic
- Code of conduct

---

## Stance System

Knights have access to the following stances:

- **Cavalry Stance** (+mounted damage)
- **Defender Stance** (+party defense)
- **Champion Stance** (+honor gain)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance cavalry]
[stance defender]
[stance champion]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Rally
- **Effect:** Buff all nearby allies
- **Cooldown:** 60s cooldown

#### Charge
- **Effect:** Mounted charge attack
- **Cooldown:** 20s cooldown

#### Challenge
- **Effect:** Force 1v1 with enemy
- **Cooldown:** 45s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Rally party before fight
2. Mounted Charge engage
3. Challenge priority target
4. Defend allies
5. Honorable combat
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Tank / Melee means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay in melee range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
