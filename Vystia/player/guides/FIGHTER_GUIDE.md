# Fighter - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Fighter
**Region:** Ironclad
**Role:** Tank
**Difficulty:** ★☆☆☆☆ (Very Easy)

---

## Overview

Weapons mastery, defensive skills, taunts, armor. Straightforward melee tank with high survivability.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 40 |
| DEX | 25 |
| INT | 15 |

### Primary Skill
**CombatMastery (ID: 76)** - Governs all Fighter abilities


---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Straightforward melee tank with high survivability

### Strengths
- High defense
- Simple mechanics
- Reliable tanking
- Weapon mastery

### Weaknesses
- Low damage
- No magic
- Limited range
- Basic gameplay

---

## Stance System

Fighters have access to the following stances:

- **Shield Stance** (+defense)
- **Sword Stance** (+damage)
- **Balance Stance** (even stats)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance shield]
[stance sword]
[stance balance]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Taunt
- **Effect:** Force enemy to attack you
- **Cooldown:** 10s cooldown

#### Shield Wall
- **Effect:** +50% defense for 10s
- **Cooldown:** 60s cooldown

#### Charge
- **Effect:** Rush to enemy and stun
- **Cooldown:** 20s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Charge to engage
2. Taunt priority targets
3. Shield Wall when low
4. Block heavy attacks
5. Maintain aggro
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Tank means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay in melee range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
