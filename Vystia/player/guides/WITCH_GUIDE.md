# Witch - Complete Gameplay Guide

**Class:** Witch
**Region:** Shadowfen
**Role:** Debuffer
**Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆ (Intermediate)

---

## Overview

Curses, hexes, debuffs, life drain. Weaken enemies while draining their life.

### Base Stats
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| STR | 15 |
| DEX | 20 |
| INT | 45 |

### Primary Skill
**Hexcraft (ID: 64)** - Governs all Witch abilities

### Spellbook
**WitchSpellbook**
- 32 spells across 8 circles
- Spawn: `[spellbook witch]` or `[sb witch]`

**Special Item:** WitchSpellbook

---

## Playstyle Philosophy

### Core Concept
Weaken enemies while draining their life

### Strengths
- Powerful debuffs
- Life drain sustain
- DoT damage
- Enemy crippling

### Weaknesses
- Slow kill speed
- Cleanse removes curses
- Setup time
- Less burst

---

## Stance System

Witchs have access to the following stances:

- **Curse Stance** (+curse power)
- **Drain Stance** (+life steal)
- **Hex Stance** (+debuff duration)

**Stance Commands:**
```
[stance curse]
[stance drain]
[stance hex]
```

---

## Abilities

#### Mass Curse
- **Effect:** Curse all nearby enemies
- **Cooldown:** 60s cooldown

#### Life Tap
- **Effect:** Convert HP to mana
- **Cooldown:** 30s cooldown

#### Doom
- **Effect:** Target dies in 10s unless healed
- **Cooldown:** 120s cooldown


---

## Combat Rotation

```
1. Apply curses first
2. Stack debuffs
3. Life drain for sustain
4. DoTs on all targets
5. Doom on priority target
```

---

## Tips & Tricks

### General Tips
1. **Know your role** - Debuffer means focusing on that aspect
2. **Use stances** - Switch stances based on situation
3. **Manage resources** - Don't spam abilities unnecessarily
4. **Position well** - Stay in melee range

### Advanced Techniques
- Combine abilities for maximum effect
- Pre-buff before engaging tough enemies
- Know when to retreat and reset
