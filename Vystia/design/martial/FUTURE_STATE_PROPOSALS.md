## Industry Archetype Standards for Martial Classes

Based on analysis of **World of Warcraft**, **Final Fantasy XIV**, **Guild Wars 2**, **Path of Exile**, **D&D 5e**:

### Core Martial Archetype Categories

| Archetype | Primary Role | Damage | Utility | Defense | Mobility | Examples |
|-----------|-------------|--------|---------|---------|----------|----------|
| **Tank** | Defense | 2 | 3-4 | 5 | 1-2 | WoW Protection Paladin, FFXIV Paladin |
| **Melee DPS** | Damage | 5 | 2-3 | 2-3 | 2-3 | WoW Warrior, FFXIV Monk |
| **Ranged DPS** | Damage | 5 | 3-4 | 1-2 | 3-4 | WoW Hunter, FFXIV Bard |
| **Burst DPS** | Damage | 5 | 1-2 | 1-2 | 2-3 | WoW Rogue, PoE Assassin |
| **Support** | Utility | 2-3 | 5 | 2-3 | 2-3 | WoW Enhancement Shaman, FFXIV Bard |
| **Healer** | Healing | 1-2 | 3-4 | 2-3 | 2-3 | WoW Priest, FFXIV White Mage |
| **Pet Class** | Summons | 2-3 | 2-3 | 1-2 | 1-2 | WoW Warlock, D&D Ranger |

## Current State Assessment

### Tank Classes (Knight, Paladin, Templar)

**Current State:**
- ✅ Good defense abilities
- ✅ Tank mechanics (taunt, shield, mitigation)
- ⚠️ May need more utility for group support
- ⚠️ May need mobility for positioning

**Future State Needs:**
- Add group support utilities (buffs, auras)
- Add mobility abilities (charge, reposition)
- Enhance threat generation mechanics

---

### Melee DPS Classes (Fighter, Barbarian, Monk)

**Current State:**
- ✅ Good damage abilities
- ✅ Stance/combo systems
- ⚠️ May need more mobility
- ⚠️ May need defensive cooldowns

**Future State Needs:**
- Add mobility abilities (dash, leap, charge)
- Add defensive cooldowns (damage reduction, immunity)
- Enhance burst windows

---

### Ranged DPS Classes (Ranger)

**Current State:**
- ✅ Ranged damage abilities
- ✅ Terrain mastery
- ⚠️ May need more mobility
- ⚠️ May need escape abilities

**Future State Needs:**
- Add mobility abilities (roll, dash, disengage)
- Add escape abilities (vanish, feign death)
- Enhance kiting mechanics

---

### Burst/Utility Classes (Rogue, Bounty Hunter)

**Current State:**
- ✅ Stealth mechanics
- ✅ Burst damage
- ⚠️ May need more utility
- ⚠️ May need defensive options

**Future State Needs:**
- Add utility abilities (traps, tracking, detection)
- Add defensive cooldowns (evasion, dodge)
- Enhance stealth mechanics

---

### Pet Classes (Beastmaster)

**Current State:**
- ✅ Pet summoning
- ✅ Bond mechanics
- ⚠️ May need pet utilities
- ⚠️ May need defensive options

**Future State Needs:**
- Add pet utilities (pet teleport, pet heal, pet buff)
- Add defensive abilities (pet tank, pet shield)
- Enhance pet synergy

---

### Support Classes (Cleric, Wizard, Alchemist, Artificer)

**Current State:**
- ✅ Support abilities
- ✅ Utility mechanics
- ⚠️ May need more group support
- ⚠️ May need mobility

**Future State Needs:**
- Add group buff abilities
- Add mobility abilities
- Enhance support mechanics

## Proposed Changes by Class

### Fighter (Melee DPS)
**Current:** Weapon master, stance dancing  
**Needs:**
- ➕ Add mobility ability (charge, leap)
- ➕ Add defensive cooldown (second wind, parry stance)
- ➕ Add utility ability (battle cry, rally)

---

### Barbarian (Burst Melee)
**Current:** Rage system, burst damage  
**Needs:**
- ➕ Add mobility ability (furious charge, leap)
- ➕ Add defensive cooldown (berserker's rage immunity)
- ➕ Add utility ability (intimidating shout)

---

### Monk (Combo DPS)
**Current:** Chi system, combo points  
**Needs:**
- ➕ Add mobility ability (flying kick, teleport)
- ➕ Add defensive cooldown (meditation, dodge)
- ➕ Add utility ability (healing touch, chi burst)

---

### Rogue (Burst/Utility)
**Current:** Stealth, combo points  
**Needs:**
- ➕ Add utility ability (detect traps, pick lock)
- ➕ Add defensive cooldown (evasion, vanish)

---

## Implementation Priority

### Phase 1: Critical PvP/Solo Fixes
1. Add mobility/escape to all classes
2. Add defensive cooldowns to all classes
3. Add self-healing to DPS classes (where appropriate)

### Phase 2: Role Fulfillment
1. Add group support to tank classes
2. Add utility to support classes
3. Enhance pet mechanics for pet classes

### Phase 3: Utility Enhancement
1. Add detection abilities
2. Add resource management
3. Enhance class-specific mechanics
