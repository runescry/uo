## Quick Links

### Pet System
- 4 pet classes: Beastmaster, Summoner, Necromancer, Artificer
- 5 power tiers: Lesser to Legendary
- Skill-based scaling

### Housing System
- 5 house sizes: Small to Castle
- 50k to 3M purchase prices
- Weekly property taxes

### Zone Control
- 4 zone types: Sanctuary, Contested, Lawless, Extreme
- PvP rules per zone
- Death penalties and loot drops

### Faction System
- 7 regional factions
- Reputation progression (Hostile to Exalted)
- Vendor discounts up to 15%

### Economy System
- Service fees (resurrection, travel, stabling)
- Gold sink mechanics
- NPC vendors (Healer, Moongate Attendant)

### Religion System
- 6 regional religions
- Piety progression (0-1000)
- Passive bonuses and devotion powers
