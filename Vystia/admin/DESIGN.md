# Vystia World Design

Complete custom world built on ServUO.

## Quest Design

### 4-Tier Structure

| Tier | Level | Purpose | Rewards |
|------|-------|---------|---------|
| Initiation | 1-30 | Learn basics | Class focus item |
| Apprentice | 30-60 | Develop identity | Tier 2 abilities |
| Journeyman | 60-90 | Advanced techniques | Legendary items |
| Master | 90+ | Class mastery | Ultimate abilities |

Each class has 12 quests (3 per tier).

---

## Faction Design

### Major Factions
- Regional factions (one per region)
- Class guilds (one per class)
- Player-joinable factions with reputation systems

## Resource System

### Magic Reagents (96)
8 unique reagents per magic school, themed to school identity.

### Crafting Materials
Regional ores, ingots, woods, leathers with unique properties.
