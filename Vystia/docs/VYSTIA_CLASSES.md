# The Classes of Vystia

## Overview
Vystia is home to 26 distinct character classes, each tied to specific regions, guilds, or covenants across the realm. These classes represent specialized training, cultural traditions, and mastery over elemental, mechanical, or arcane forces.

---

## Class Directory

### Frosthold Region
#### **Barbarian** - Frosthold Berserkers
**Regional Affiliation:** Frosthold Mountains
**Guild/Order:** The Berserker Clans of Winterpeak

**Description:**
Savage warriors who channel the primal fury of the frozen north. Frosthold Berserkers are renowned for their incredible endurance in sub-zero temperatures and their ability to enter battle rages that make them nearly unstoppable forces on the battlefield.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Strength, Constitution
- **Combat Style:** Two-handed weapons, dual wielding
- **Armor Preference:** Medium armor (furs, reinforced leather)
- **Special Resource:** Rage/Fury

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Frost Fury** - Channel ice magic into melee attacks
- **Berserker Rage** - Temporary immunity to cold and increased damage
- **Avalanche Strike** - AoE ground slam that creates ice shards
- **Winter's Endurance** - Passive cold resistance and HP regeneration in cold environments

**Lore:**
The Berserker Clans trace their lineage to the first settlers of Frosthold who survived the Great Freeze through sheer willpower and adaptation. They believe that rage is a gift from the Ice Father, allowing mortals to survive where magic fails.

---

#### **Beastmaster** - Pack Leaders of Frosthold
**Regional Affiliation:** Frosthold Wilderness
**Guild/Order:** The Winter Pack Brotherhood

**Description:**
Masters of arctic beast taming and pack tactics. Beastmasters bond with wolves, bears, and other predators of the frozen wastes, coordinating attacks and sharing primal instincts with their companions.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Dexterity, Wisdom, Animal Lore
- **Combat Style:** Ranged combat with pet coordination
- **Armor Preference:** Light/medium armor
- **Special Resource:** Pack Bond meter

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Call of the Wild** - Summon arctic beasts to aid in combat
- **Pack Tactics** - Bonus damage when fighting alongside tamed creatures
- **Feral Bond** - Share senses and abilities with bonded companion
- **Alpha's Command** - Control multiple creatures simultaneously

**Lore:**
The Pack Leaders learned their craft from observing wolf behavior during the long winters. They claim to speak the "Old Tongue" of beasts, a language that predates human settlement in Frosthold.

---

### Emberlands Region
#### **Sorcerer** - Elemental Savants of The Emberlands
**Regional Affiliation:** Volcanic Emberlands
**Guild/Order:** The Pyroclast Conclave

**Description:**
Elemental magic users who draw power directly from the volcanic heart of the Emberlands. Unlike Wizards who study magic academically, Sorcerers possess innate magical bloodlines tied to elemental forces.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Intelligence, Charisma
- **Magic School:** Evocation, Transmutation
- **Armor Preference:** Robes, enchanted cloth
- **Special Resource:** Elemental Resonance

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Magma Bolt** - High-damage fire projectile
- **Volcanic Shield** - Absorb damage and return fire damage to attackers
- **Pyroclastic Surge** - AoE explosion centered on caster
- **Elemental Embodiment** - Transform into pure fire form temporarily

**Lore:**
The Pyroclast Conclave believes that true sorcerers are descendants of those who survived the First Eruption - a cataclysm that reshaped the Emberlands. Their blood carries traces of elemental fire, manifesting as natural magical talent.

---

### The Whispering Sands (Desert)
#### **Ranger** - Dune Striders of The Whispering Sands
**Regional Affiliation:** Sunbaked Desert
**Guild/Order:** The Sandstrider Scouts

**Description:**
Expert trackers and survivalists who navigate the treacherous desert using ancient wayfinding techniques. Dune Striders are masters of ranged combat, guerrilla tactics, and desert survival.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Dexterity, Wisdom, Tracking
- **Combat Style:** Archery, light weapons
- **Armor Preference:** Light armor (desert wraps, reinforced cloth)
- **Special Resource:** Focus/Precision

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Sandstorm Arrow** - Projectile creates blinding sand cloud on impact
- **Desert Camouflage** - Stealth bonus in sandy/arid terrain
- **Oasis Sense** - Detect water sources and track creatures in desert
- **Sunburst Shot** - Fire-enchanted arrow that explodes in radiant light

**Lore:**
The Sandstrider Scouts claim to hear the "whispers" of the desert - subtle shifts in wind and sand that warn of danger or guide them to hidden oases. They serve as guides, scouts, and protectors of trade routes through the wastes.

---

#### **Illusionist** - Mirage Weavers of The Whispering Sands
**Regional Affiliation:** Desert Borderlands
**Guild/Order:** The Veiled Circle

**Description:**
Masters of deception and illusion magic, inspired by the desert's natural mirages. Illusionists manipulate perception, create false images, and confuse enemies with elaborate phantasms.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Intelligence, Charisma
- **Magic School:** Illusion, Enchantment
- **Armor Preference:** Light armor, enchanted robes
- **Special Resource:** Mirage Energy

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Phantom Duplicate** - Create illusory copies of self
- **Desert Mirage** - Create false terrain or structures
- **Blinding Veil** - Area effect that blinds and disorients enemies
- **Reality Shatter** - Dismiss illusions or break enchantments

**Lore:**
The Veiled Circle was founded by a nomadic tribe who learned to weaponize the desert's natural illusions. They believe that reality itself is malleable, and that the desert's mirages are glimpses into parallel possibilities.

### Shadowfen Region
#### **Witch** - Covens of Shadowfen
**Regional Affiliation:** Shadowfen Swamps
**Guild/Order:** The Bog Witch Covens

**Description:**
Practitioners of dark nature magic, curses, and hexcraft. Shadowfen Witches draw power from the murky waters and decaying vegetation of the swamp, specializing in poison, disease, and necromantic rituals.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Intelligence, Wisdom
- **Magic School:** Necromancy, Curses, Nature Magic
- **Armor Preference:** Light armor, ritual garb
- **Special Resource:** Hex Power

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Bog Curse** - DoT that reduces enemy stats
- **Swamp Familiar** - Summon amphibious creature companion
- **Poison Mist** - AoE poison cloud
- **Death Bloom** - Cause plants to rapidly grow and ensnare enemies

**Lore:**
The Bog Witch Covens are ancient, predating the current kingdoms. They claim descent from the original inhabitants of Shadowfen who learned to commune with the spirits of decay and rebirth. Each coven guards secret grimoires passed down through centuries.

---

#### **Warlock** - Covenant of the Hidden Eye
**Regional Affiliation:** Shadowfen Depths
**Guild/Order:** The Hidden Eye Covenant

**Description:**
Mages who have made pacts with otherworldly entities in exchange for dark powers. Warlocks channel eldritch energy through their patrons, wielding curses, drains, and forbidden magic.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Intelligence, Charisma
- **Magic School:** Dark Magic, Conjuration
- **Armor Preference:** Reinforced robes, light armor
- **Special Resource:** Pact Energy

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Eldritch Blast** - Force damage beam
- **Life Drain** - Steal HP from enemies
- **Summon Patron** - Call forth aspect of otherworldly master
- **Dark Pact** - Sacrifice HP for temporary power boost

**Lore:**
The Hidden Eye Covenant operates in secret, its members bound by blood oaths to entities from beyond the veil. Their patrons - Shadow Lords, Void Entities, and Ancient Evils - grant power in exchange for service and souls.

### Verdantpeak Region
#### **Druid** - Guardians of Verdantpeak
**Regional Affiliation:** Verdantpeak Forests
**Guild/Order:** The Emerald Circle

**Description:**
Nature priests who protect the ancient forests and maintain the balance between civilization and wilderness. Druids can shapeshift, commune with animals, and wield primal nature magic.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Wisdom, Constitution
- **Magic School:** Nature Magic, Transmutation
- **Armor Preference:** Medium armor (bark, wood, leather)
- **Special Resource:** Nature's Blessing

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Wild Shape** - Transform into animals (wolf, bear, bird)
- **Entangling Roots** - Immobilize enemies with animated vines
- **Nature's Wrath** - Call lightning or summon storms
- **Regeneration** - Healing over time for self or allies

**Lore:**
The Emerald Circle traces its origins to the first Treant, the Ancient of Verdantpeak. Druids undergo a sacred ritual where they bond with a living tree, gaining the ability to hear the "Green Song" - the collective consciousness of the forest.

---

#### **Alchemist** - Brewmasters of the Verdant Isles
**Regional Affiliation:** Verdantpeak/Coastal Regions
**Guild/Order:** The Brewmaster's Guild

**Description:**
Masters of potion-crafting, transmutation, and alchemical combat. Alchemists use volatile concoctions, bombs, and mutagens to control the battlefield and enhance their allies.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Intelligence, Dexterity
- **Combat Style:** Thrown weapons, support magic
- **Armor Preference:** Light/medium armor
- **Special Resource:** Reagent Stock

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Alchemical Bomb** - Throw explosive flask (fire, ice, acid, etc.)
- **Transmutation** - Temporarily alter object properties
- **Mutagen** - Drink potion to enhance stats or gain abilities
- **Instant Brew** - Craft potions rapidly during combat

**Lore:**
The Brewmaster's Guild began as a order of herbalists and apothecaries but evolved into a prestigious academy. They claim to possess the original formulas of the Emerald Elixir - a legendary potion said to grant immortality.

### Crystal Barrens Region
#### **Oracle** - Seers of the Crystal Barrens
**Regional Affiliation:** Crystal Barrens
**Guild/Order:** The Prismatic Seers

**Description:**
Prophets and diviners who read the future in crystalline formations. Oracles wield precognitive magic, fate manipulation, and psychic abilities granted by the sentient crystals of the Barrens.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Wisdom, Charisma
- **Magic School:** Divination, Psychic
- **Armor Preference:** Enchanted robes
- **Special Resource:** Foresight

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Glimpse Future** - Brief precognition to dodge attacks or predict enemy moves
- **Fate Weave** - Alter probability for critical hits or lucky events
- **Psychic Bolt** - Mental damage attack
- **Crystal Communion** - Scry distant locations or read memories

**Lore:**
The Prismatic Seers believe the crystals of the Barrens are fragments of a cosmic entity that exists across all timelines. By meditating within crystal formations, Oracles can perceive branching futures and guide fate itself.

### Ironclad Region
#### **Artificer** - The Technoguild Artisans
**Regional Affiliation:** Ironclad Empire
**Guild/Order:** The Technoguild

**Description:**
Engineers and inventors who blend magic with machinery. Artificers create clockwork constructs, enchanted weapons, and steam-powered devices that blur the line between magic and technology.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Intelligence, Dexterity
- **Combat Style:** Ranged weapons, constructs
- **Armor Preference:** Medium/heavy armor (reinforced with gadgets)
- **Special Resource:** Invention Points

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Deploy Turret** - Place automated weapon emplacement
- **Mechanical Companion** - Build and control clockwork pet
- **Gadget Bomb** - Throw device with various effects (EMP, net, flash)
- **Emergency Shield** - Activate defensive barrier generator

**Lore:**
The Technoguild is the heart of Ironclad innovation, founded when ancient dwarven engineering met human ingenuity. Artificers believe that magic is simply energy that can be harnessed through proper machinery - a philosophy that has revolutionized warfare and industry.

---

#### **Fighter** - Ironclad Legionnaires
**Regional Affiliation:** Ironclad Empire
**Guild/Order:** The Imperial Legion

**Description:**
Elite soldiers trained in disciplined combat tactics and weapon mastery. Legionnaires form the backbone of Ironclad's military might, wielding advanced weaponry and steam-powered armor.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Strength, Constitution
- **Combat Style:** All weapons, specialized in formation fighting
- **Armor Preference:** Heavy armor (plate, steam-powered exoskeletons)
- **Special Resource:** Stamina/Discipline

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Shield Wall** - Defensive formation with damage reduction
- **Weapon Mastery** - Switch fighting styles for different situations
- **Power Strike** - High-damage single target attack
- **Ironclad Stance** - Reduce incoming damage, increase threat generation

**Lore:**
The Imperial Legion traces its traditions to the founding of Ironclad, when disparate clans united under a single banner. Every Legionnaire swears the Forge Oath, pledging to defend the Empire until their armor rusts and their blade shatters.

---

#### **Templar** - Justicars of the Ironclad Empire
**Regional Affiliation:** Ironclad Empire
**Guild/Order:** The Order of the Iron Justicars

**Description:**
Holy warriors who serve as judge, jury, and executioner in Ironclad's military justice system. Templars blend martial prowess with divine magic, smiting criminals and defending the innocent.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Strength, Wisdom
- **Combat Style:** Melee combat with holy magic
- **Armor Preference:** Heavy armor
- **Special Resource:** Conviction

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Judgement Strike** - Holy damage attack that marks targets
- **Divine Shield** - Protect allies with blessed barrier
- **Aura of Justice** - Buff nearby allies with damage and resistance
- **Execution** - Instant kill on low-health marked targets

**Lore:**
The Iron Justicars were created to root out corruption within Ironclad's government and military. They answer only to the High Council and are empowered to pass judgment on anyone, regardless of rank. Their armor bears the Scales of Balance, symbol of impartial justice.

---

#### **Monk** - Gearwheel Monastery's Monks
**Regional Affiliation:** Ironclad Border Regions
**Guild/Order:** The Gearwheel Monastery

**Description:**
Martial artists who have mastered the harmony between flesh and machine. Gearwheel Monks augment their bodies with clockwork enhancements, achieving superhuman speed and precision.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Dexterity, Wisdom
- **Combat Style:** Unarmed, martial arts, light weapons
- **Armor Preference:** Unarmored or light armor
- **Special Resource:** Ki/Gear Energy

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Flurry of Blows** - Rapid melee attack combo
- **Clockwork Reflexes** - Dodge attacks with mechanical precision
- **Iron Fist** - Unarmed attacks count as magical/metal weapons
- **Perfect Balance** - Immune to knockdown, enhanced mobility

**Lore:**
The Gearwheel Monastery was founded by a disgraced Artificer who sought to perfect the body rather than craft external machines. Monks undergo augmentation rituals, embedding clockwork components into their muscles and bones, achieving perfect synchronization of flesh and metal.

---

### Skyreach Region
#### **Ice Mage** - Frostweavers of Winterguard
**Regional Affiliation:** Skyreach Peak (border with Frosthold)
**Guild/Order:** The Winterguard Spire

**Description:**
Specialized mages who have mastered ice and cold magic. Unlike generalist Wizards, Ice Mages focus exclusively on frost-based spells, achieving unparalleled mastery over winter's fury.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Intelligence, Constitution
- **Magic School:** Evocation (Ice), Conjuration
- **Armor Preference:** Enchanted robes
- **Special Resource:** Frost Power

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Ice Lance** - Piercing ice projectile
- **Frozen Fortress** - Create walls of ice for defense
- **Blizzard** - Large AoE snow storm with slow and damage
- **Permafrost** - Cover ground in ice, slowing enemies

**Lore:**
The Winterguard Spire was established on the highest peak of Skyreach, where eternal winter meets the clouds. Ice Mages train in subzero temperatures, learning to hear the "Song of Winter" - a mystical resonance that allows them to command ice itself.

**Regional Affiliation:** ShadowVoid
**Guild/Order:** The Twilight Cabal

**Description:**
Masters of death magic who command undead armies and drain life force. Necromancers walk the line between life and death, drawing power from the void between worlds.

**Core Attributes:**

- **Magic School:** Necromancy, Dark Magic
- **Armor Preference:** Robes, bone armor

**Signature Abilities:**

- **Life Drain** - Steal HP from enemies

The Twilight Cabal operates from hidden sanctums in ShadowVoid, studying the nature of death and undeath. They claim to serve a higher purpose - preparing mortals for the "Final Twilight" when death will claim all living things. Many kingdoms have outlawed Necromancy, driving practitioners into hiding.

### Underwater/Forgotten Depths Region
#### **Summoner** - Arcane Summoners of the Forgotten Depths
**Regional Affiliation:** Deep Ocean/Coastal Cities
**Guild/Order:** The Depthcaller Assembly

**Description:**
Mages who specialize in conjuring creatures from other planes and the ocean depths. Summoners maintain mental links with multiple entities, coordinating them in complex battle strategies.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Intelligence, Charisma
- **Magic School:** Conjuration, Binding
- **Armor Preference:** Light armor, enchanted robes
- **Special Resource:** Summoning Power

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Summon Elemental** - Call water, air, earth, or fire elemental
- **Abyssal Servant** - Summon deep sea creature
- **Binding Circle** - Trap and control summoned entities
- **Mass Summon** - Call multiple minor creatures at once

**Lore:**
The Depthcaller Assembly was founded by survivors of a sunken city who learned to communicate with the denizens of the deep. They discovered that the ocean depths are thin places where other planes touch our world, making summoning easier and more powerful near water.

### Sunken Isles Region
#### **Bounty Hunter** - Crypt Trackers of the Sunken Isles
**Regional Affiliation:** Sunken Isles/Coastal Regions
**Guild/Order:** The Crypt Tracker Guild

**Description:**
Professional trackers and monster hunters who specialize in hunting undead, aberrations, and dangerous criminals. Bounty Hunters use traps, specialized weapons, and alchemical preparations to take down their quarry.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Dexterity, Wisdom, Tracking
- **Combat Style:** Ranged and melee, traps
- **Armor Preference:** Medium armor
- **Special Resource:** Hunter's Mark

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Mark Target** - Designate enemy for bonus damage
- **Trap Placement** - Deploy snares, explosive traps, nets
- **Silver Strike** - Attacks ignore undead resistances
- **Hunter's Quarry** - Track marked target across any distance

**Lore:**
The Crypt Tracker Guild was formed to deal with the undead plague that rose from the Sunken Isles. They maintain detailed bestiaries, contract boards, and reward systems. A Tracker's reputation is measured by their "Tally" - the count of monsters slain and contracts completed.

### Glimmering Archipelago Region
#### **Knight** - Guardians of the Glimmering Archipelago
**Regional Affiliation:** Island Kingdoms
**Guild/Order:** The Order of the Azure Tide

**Description:**
Noble warriors who have sworn oaths to protect the innocent and uphold codes of chivalry. Knights of the Azure Tide are elite cavalry and naval defenders who patrol the waters between islands.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Strength, Charisma
- **Combat Style:** Mounted combat, lance and sword
- **Armor Preference:** Heavy armor (plate)
- **Special Resource:** Honor/Valor

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Charge** - Mounted attack with knockdown
- **Noble Shield** - Protect allies by taking damage for them
- **Challenge** - Force enemy to attack you instead of allies
- **Rally** - Buff nearby allies with morale boost

**Lore:**
The Order of the Azure Tide claims descent from the first sailors who settled the Glimmering Archipelago. Every Knight undergoes the Trial of Tides - a dangerous voyage across treacherous waters to prove their worth. They believe honor is the only treasure worth dying for.

### The Wilderlands (Multi-Region)
#### **Shaman** - Spiritcallers of The Wilderlands
**Regional Affiliation:** Tribal lands across multiple regions
**Guild/Order:** The Circle of Ancestors

**Description:**
Spiritual leaders who commune with ancestral spirits and elemental forces. Shamans serve as healers, advisors, and intermediaries between the mortal world and the spirit realm.

**Core Attributes:**

- **Magic School:** Nature Magic, Spirit Magic, Healing
- **Armor Preference:** Light/medium armor (ritual garb)
- **Special Resource:** Spirit Energy

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Spirit Wolves** - Summon ancestral spirit guardians
- **Elemental Totem** - Place totem that buffs allies or damages enemies
- **Ancestral Guidance** - Temporarily channel ancient warrior spirits
- **Healing Rain** - AoE healing over time

**Lore:**
The Circle of Ancestors transcends regional boundaries, uniting shamans from various tribes and cultures. They maintain oral histories spanning thousands of years, believing that the dead never truly leave - they simply join the Great Spirit that flows through all living things.

### Multi-Regional/Academy
#### **Wizard** - Scholars of the Arcane Academy
**Regional Affiliation:** Multiple (Academy has branches in major cities)
**Guild/Order:** The Arcane Academy

**Description:**
Generalist mages who study magic as a scholarly discipline. Wizards master multiple schools of magic through intense study, memorization, and experimentation, unlike Sorcerers with innate power.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Intelligence, Wisdom
- **Magic School:** All schools (specialization available)
- **Armor Preference:** Robes, enchanted clothing
- **Special Resource:** Mana, Spell Slots

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Spellbook** - Prepare spells from extensive learned repertoire
- **Counterspell** - Interrupt enemy magic
- **Ritual Casting** - Cast powerful spells outside of combat
- **Metamagic** - Modify spell effects (extend duration, increase range, etc.)

**Lore:**
The Arcane Academy was founded 500 years ago by an alliance of mages seeking to formalize magical education. They maintain the Great Library - a repository of magical knowledge spanning millennia. Academy graduates are recognized across all kingdoms as certified practitioners of the arcane arts.

---

#### **Cleric** - Priests of the Elemental Balance
**Regional Affiliation:** Multi-regional (temples in all major cities)
**Guild/Order:** The Church of Elemental Harmony

**Description:**
Holy casters who channel divine power from the elemental gods. Clerics serve as healers, spiritual guides, and warriors against undead and demonic forces.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Wisdom, Charisma
- **Magic School:** Divine Magic, Healing
- **Armor Preference:** Medium/heavy armor
- **Special Resource:** Divine Power

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Healing Touch** - Direct healing spell
- **Turn Undead** - Force undead to flee or destroy them
- **Divine Smite** - Channel divine energy into weapon attacks
- **Sanctuary** - Create zone where combat is prevented

**Lore:**
The Church of Elemental Harmony teaches that all magic stems from six elemental gods (Fire, Water, Earth, Air, Light, Shadow) who must remain in balance. Clerics choose a primary deity but honor all six, acting as mediators and peacekeepers between warring elemental factions.

---

#### **Paladin** - Knights of the Forge Pact
**Regional Affiliation:** Multi-regional (originated in Ironclad)
**Guild/Order:** The Forge Pact

**Description:**
Holy warriors who combine martial prowess with divine magic. Paladins take sacred oaths binding them to codes of conduct, gaining divine powers in exchange for unwavering dedication to their cause.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Strength, Charisma
- **Combat Style:** Melee with divine magic support
- **Armor Preference:** Heavy armor
- **Special Resource:** Holy Power

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Lay on Hands** - Touch-based healing
- **Divine Weapon** - Enchant weapon with holy damage
- **Aura of Protection** - Passive buff to nearby allies
- **Smite Evil** - Massive damage against evil creatures

**Lore:**
The Forge Pact was created when Ironclad's Templars allied with the Church of Elemental Harmony. Paladins swear to uphold justice, protect the weak, and fight corruption wherever it appears. Breaking their oath strips them of divine power, creating "Fallen Paladins" - warriors consumed by guilt and shame.

---

#### **Bard** - Steam Harmonic Virtuosos
**Regional Affiliation:** Multi-regional (performance-based, travel widely)
**Guild/Order:** The Virtuoso's College

**Description:**
Performers and musicians who weave magic through song, story, and artistry. Bards use sonic magic, inspiration, and battlefield control to support allies and demoralize enemies.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Charisma, Dexterity
- **Magic School:** Enchantment, Illusion
- **Armor Preference:** Light/medium armor
- **Special Resource:** Inspiration

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Inspiring Song** - Buff allies with performance
- **Sonic Blast** - Damage enemies with pure sound
- **Charm** - Enchant enemy to fight for you
- **Counter-performance** - Interrupt enemy spellcasting with discordant notes

**Lore:**
The Virtuoso's College has chapters in every major city, teaching that music is the fundamental vibration of reality itself. The "Steam Harmonic" tradition emerged in Ironclad, where bards learned to harmonize with the rhythmic sounds of machinery, creating a new school of industrial magic-music fusion.

---

#### **Enchanter** - Sigilmasters of the Mystic Canyons
**Regional Affiliation:** Remote canyon monasteries (border regions)
**Guild/Order:** The Sigil Covenant

**Description:**
Mages who specialize in permanent magical enhancements, runes, and enchantments. Enchanters can imbue objects with power, create magical wards, and manipulate existing enchantments.

**Core Attributes:**
- **Primary Stats:** Intelligence, Dexterity (for precise rune-work)
- **Magic School:** Enchantment, Abjuration
- **Armor Preference:** Light armor
- **Special Resource:** Runic Power

**Signature Abilities:**
- **Enchant Weapon** - Permanently enhance equipment
- **Sigil of Protection** - Draw protective rune on surface
- **Dispel Magic** - Remove enchantments
- **Runic Trap** - Create magical trap activated by conditions

**Lore:**
The Sigil Covenant guards ancient knowledge of the First Runes - symbols of power carved into reality itself at the dawn of creation. Sigilmasters believe that all magic is simply writing in the language of creation, and mastering this language grants dominion over reality.

## Class Balance Philosophy

### Role Distribution

**Tank/Defender (6):**
- Barbarian, Fighter, Knight, Paladin, Templar, Monk*

**Healer/Support (5):**
- Cleric, Druid, Bard, Shaman, Alchemist*

**Melee DPS (5):**
- Barbarian*, Monk*, Bounty Hunter, Knight*, Paladin*

**Ranged DPS (6):**
- Ranger, Artificer, Bounty Hunter*, Ice Mage, Wizard, Sorcerer

**Caster DPS (10):**
- Sorcerer*, Ice Mage*, Wizard*, Warlock, Necromancer, Witch, Enchanter, Illusionist

**Summoner/Pet Class (5):**
- Summoner, Druid*, Beastmaster, Necromancer*, Warlock*

**Utility/Control (7):**
- Bard*, Enchanter*, Illusionist*, Oracle, Shaman*, Witch*, Alchemist*

*Classes marked with asterisk can fulfill multiple roles

## Class Conflicts and Alliances

### Natural Allies
- **Paladins ↔ Clerics ↔ Templars** - Holy trinity of divine warriors
- **Druids ↔ Rangers ↔ Shamans** - Nature protectors
- **Artificers ↔ Wizards ↔ Enchanters** - Scholars of magic
- **Barbarians ↔ Beastmasters** - Frosthold warriors
- **Knights ↔ Fighters** - Military allies

### Rivalries
- **Necromancers vs. Clerics/Paladins** - Undead vs. holy powers
- **Artificers vs. Druids** - Technology vs. nature
- **Sorcerers vs. Wizards** - Innate power vs. learned magic
- **Witches vs. Clerics** - Dark magic vs. divine order
- **Templars vs. Warlocks** - Law vs. forbidden pacts

### Uneasy Truces
- **Necromancers ↔ Witches** - Both use dark arts, different philosophies
- **Illusionists ↔ Enchanters** - Competing magical traditions
- **Bounty Hunters ↔ Rogues** - Hunters vs. hunted
- **Ice Mages ↔ Sorcerers** - Specialized vs. generalist casters
